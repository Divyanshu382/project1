What do the posting formats mean?
1/24: Your ad will be displayed in the channel for 24 hours, with 1 hour at the top of the feed. 2/48: Your ad will be displayed in the channel for 48 hours, with 2 hours at the top of the feed. 3/72: Your ad will be displayed in the channel for 72 hours, with 3 hours at the top of the feed. 7 days: Your ad will be displayed in the channel for 7 days, with 3 hours at the top of the feed. Forwards: A post from the advertised channel is republished in another channel under the 1/24 format, with a guaranteed minimum top retention of 1 hour. Native: A post created by a channel author and agreed upon with the client. The ad is displayed for 7 days with 1 hour at the top of the feed.
What happens if a channel refuses to place my ad?
If the owner of the channel or chat (group) refuses to post your advertisement, the funds will be returned to your balance within 24 hours. If you have opted for Autoreplace, our system will automatically select an alternative channel or chat (group) within the same price range, and you will be notified in your personal account.
